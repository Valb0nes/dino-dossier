Dreadnoughtus: Dino Dossier

Fossil information

After four expeditions the entire team had found about 145 bones and one singular tooth. These 145 bones included 112 from a bigger Dreadnoughtus and about 30 bones from a smaller second Dreadnoughtus. In all of those bones most of the vertebrae has been found which includes the tail, the neck, the scapula, multiple ribs, part of the jaw, a couple toes, and a claw. The entire skeleton is only about 45.3% completed and that includes the skull.
